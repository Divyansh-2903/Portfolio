## 🗂️ Complete Section Checklist

### 01 — Navbar
- Your name/logo on the left in a mono or bold sans font
- Links: `Work · Services · About · Contact` — clean, minimal, 4 max
- A **green pulsing dot + "Available for Projects"** badge on the right side — this is visible on every scroll position
- Glassmorphism blur background on scroll (transparent when at top)
- On mobile: hamburger that opens a **fullscreen overlay menu** with big type

***

### 02 — Hero Section *(Most Critical)*
- **Headline formula:** Big, bold, two-line statement. Not your name — an *idea*. Example: `"I BUILD DIGITAL EXPERIENCES."` with "EXPERIENCES" in your orange accent
- Sub-line: `Fullstack Developer · Video Editor · Content Strategist — Based in Jaipur, India`
- **Your name** appears smaller, below — not the headline
- Two CTAs side by side: `"See My Work"` (solid orange/violet button) + `"Let's Talk"` (ghost outline)
- **Spline 3D element** — a floating abstract 3D object on the right side (orbiting rings, holographic laptop, or glowing geometric shape) that **tracks your cursor**
- Subtle **animated grid/dot texture** in the background
- A **skills ticker/marquee** at the very bottom of the hero scrolling: `REACT · NEXT.JS · NODE.JS · VIDEO EDITING · MOTION GRAPHICS · AFTER EFFECTS · DIGITAL MARKETING · TYPESCRIPT ·` — repeating, slow scroll

***

### 03 — Social Proof Bar *(Steal from Nikola)*
A full-width strip right below the hero — **no section title, just raw numbers:**
- `5+ International Clients` · `3 Products Launched` · `2+ Years Building` · `10+ Projects Delivered`
- Simple, monospaced font, separated by `·` dividers
- This section takes 5 seconds to read and does massive psychological heavy lifting

***

### 04 — Services / The Arsenal *(Your Existing Idea, Upgraded)*
Keep your `THE ARSENAL` naming — it's unique. But change the layout:
- **Bento grid of 6 cards** — asymmetric sizes, not equal boxes
- Each card contains: service name in bold, 2-line description, tools used as pill badges (like `React` `Next.js` `Figma`), and a 3D/Lottie icon
- On hover: card border glows in orange, icon animates, background shifts subtly
- Cards:
    - `Fullstack Development` — React, Next.js, Node.js, Supabase, Vercel
    - `Video Editing` — Premiere Pro, After Effects, CapCut
    - `Motion Graphics` — After Effects, Spline, Lottie
    - `Digital Marketing` — SEO, Content Strategy, LinkedIn Growth
    - `UI/UX Design` — Figma, Framer, Canva
    - `SaaS Products` — OneClipHub, Browser Extensions, Web Apps

***

### 05 — Selected Works *(Steal from Andy)*
This is where Andy wins — **don't just show thumbnails, show case studies:**
- **3–4 featured projects max** — quality over quantity
- Each project card: full-bleed background image/video, project name, one-line description, tech stack pills, `"View Case Study →"` button
- On hover: **the card lifts with a 3D tilt effect** (CSS perspective transform) and the background image zooms slightly
- **OneClipHub** as the hero project — full case study page: Problem → Solution → Tech Stack → Screenshots → Results
- Layout: one large wide card on top, two smaller cards below (editorial magazine grid)
- Case study pages are their own routes: `/work/onecliphub`, `/work/grindxgrowth` etc.

***

### 06 — About Section *(Humanize, Steal from Both)*
Not a wall of text. Use a **split bento layout:**
- Left: Your photo (high quality, not a selfie — shoot against a plain wall with good lighting), or a **3D avatar via ReadyPlayerMe**
- Right top: 2–3 punchy lines about who you are — written like a human, not a LinkedIn bio
- Right bottom: **4 personality stat cards** — `21 Projects Built` / `5+ Countries Worked With` / `3 Products Launched` / `Currently: 1st Year BTech`
- A subtle **"Currently Learning"** strip: `After Effects · DSA · Advanced Node.js`
- One honest line at the bottom: *"I'm a 19-year-old builder from Jaipur who takes client work as seriously as personal projects."* — authenticity converts

***

### 07 — Testimonials *(Full Steal from Nikola — His #1 Weapon)*
Nikola has 40+ testimonials. You may only have 3–5 right now — **that's enough to start:**
- **Scrolling marquee** of testimonial cards — two rows, one going left, one going right (like seen on top SaaS sites)
- Each card: client name, their role/country, star rating, 2-3 line quote
- Even 3 strong testimonials from your US/Canada video editing clients here will outperform zero
- Section label: `WHAT CLIENTS SAY` or `TRUSTED BY`

***

### 08 — The Channel *(Your YouTube/Content — Keep It)*
Your existing section is good in concept, just needs cleaner execution:
- Show **3 latest videos** as thumbnail cards with play button overlay
- On click: opens a **lightbox video player** — don't redirect to YouTube, keep them on your site
- Section headline: `"I DOCUMENT THE BUILD"` — positions you as a build-in-public creator

***

### 09 — Tech Stack / Tools I Use
Nikola does this per-service. You should also have a **standalone visual section:**
- Logo grid of every tool you use — React, Next.js, Figma, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Supabase, Vercel, Tailwind etc.
- On hover: each logo glows in its brand color
- Simple, clean, scannable — clients and recruiters both love this

***

### 10 — FAQ Section *(Full Steal from Nikola)*
This is the **most underrated conversion section** on any portfolio. It removes objections before a client voices them:
- `"What's your typical project timeline?"` → 2–4 weeks for most web projects
- `"Do you work with international clients?"` → Yes, currently serving US and Canada based clients
- `"What's your rate?"` → Starting from $X / project or hourly
- `"Do you offer revisions?"` → Yes, X rounds included
- `"Are you available for long-term contracts?"` → Open to retainers and ongoing work
- Use an **accordion expand/collapse** interaction — clean and space-efficient

***

### 11 — Contact / CTA *(Upgraded from Both)*
- Giant headline: `"LET'S BUILD SOMETHING."` — keep this from your current design, it's 🔥
- **Two columns:** left = contact form (Name, Email, Budget range dropdown, Project type, Message), right = direct links
- Direct links: `📧 Email` · `💼 LinkedIn` · `📱 WhatsApp` · `🐙 GitHub`
- **Calendly embed** below the form — "Or skip the form, book a 15-min call directly"
- Response time notice: `"I typically respond within 24 hours"`

***

### 12 — Footer
- Your name large, centered
- Copyright + year (auto-updated via JS)
- Links: Privacy · Work · GitHub · LinkedIn
- A one-line **status indicator**: `🟢 Currently open to freelance — Updated March 2026`

## ⚡ Global Elements (Apply Everywhere)

- **Custom cursor** — glowing orange dot, grows on hover over buttons
- **Scroll progress bar** — thin orange line at very top of viewport
- **Page transition animations** — fade + slight upward slide between routes
- **GSAP ScrollTrigger** on every section — elements slide/fade in as you scroll
- **3D tilt effect on project cards** — CSS `perspective` + JS mouse tracking
- **Sticky "Available" CTA** — appears after 40% scroll on right edge: `"Hire Me →"`
- **Loading screen** — your name animates in, max 1.5 seconds, then page reveals
